Accomodations

A block of rooms has been reserved at The Standard Hotel located on the bay side of Miami Beach. If you're looking for a serene yet hip environment complete with mud baths and a top notch spa, this is the place for you!

We also recommend The Surfcomber located on bustling Collins Avenue on the Miami South Beach strip. If you're looking for a beachside boutique hotel that's in the middle of all the action, this is your place.

Please be aware the annual Miami Food Festival falls on the weekend of our wedding. Hotels will be fully booked so please reserve your room soon!
