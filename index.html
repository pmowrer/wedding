Accomodations

Things To Do

Brette & Patrick's Local Favorites

Dining

Juvia

Chic atmosphere

Serving Japanese fusion with sweeping views of Miami. We love the bento boxes at lunch!

Pub Belly Sushi

Casual atmosphere

Sushi in a whole new way. Think "BLT" sushi rolls with truffle oil.

Beachcraft at the 1Hotel

Formal yet restrained atmosphere

Tom Colicchio's elegant beachside grill offering seasonal American farm-to-table fare. Brette never misses a visit when in town (they know her by name).

Drinks

Broken Shaker Bar at The Freehand Hotel

Informal atmosphere

This is for the young at heart. Expect a hipster crowd, sipping on infused cocktails, looking aloof in the backyard setting of The Freehand Hotel/Hostel.

Bodega

Informal atmosphere

Find this hidden bar behind a taco stand. The cocktails are surprisingly wonderful. Get there early as it gets packed around 10pm.

The Gayle Cocktail Club

Old School Cool atmosphere

For those who are serious about their libations, head to the Gayle. Grab a cocktail, a light snack, enjoy the live music, and even a cigar on their street-side patio bar.

Activities

Pérez Art Museum Miami

The newly opened museum features modern and contemporary art of the 20th and 21st centuries. Stunning architecture and cafe.

Green Monkey Yoga

Brette's favorite spot for vinyasa yoga. After class pop by Panther Coffee for a caffeine fix.

Wynwood

Walk through Wynwood neighborhood and take in the mural art that covers virtually every surface. Pop by Wynwood Kitchen & Bar for a casual bite.

Miami Food & Wine Festival

Visit sobefest.com if you want to join in the fun. Be aware tickets are required for most events.

Shopping

Alchemist

Located just off Lincoln Road you can find this ultramodern clothing store tucked away on the 5th floor of a parking garage (yes! you heard right). Super modern clothing from the likes of Rick Owens. More for looking than shopping!

BASE

Hip, irreverent lifestyle goods ranging from men's clothing to fragrance.

Miami Design District

If you’re looking to enjoy a stroll through a beautiful neighborhood peppered with shops, art and architecture head to the Design District.
